Screenshot

This is the current image or information on the broadcaster's phone screen. screenshots are required by MAA account department from the broadcaster to prepare claims, Screenshot is the only evidence that adverts have been shared. below are types of Screenshot used in MAA operations. Broadcasters should ensure that all features as shown on each screenshot are captured in their screenshot, when sending them accordingly.

Type Of Screenshot

EOC (Evidence Of Contacts)

This is the screenshot showing the numbers of contacts on a Braodcaster phone.

EOB (Evidence Of Broadcast)

This is the screenshot showing that a broadcast has been made, the following feature should be capture in EOB, Number of recipients (Audience), the advert shown in the media bar, your MAA reference number ie MAA-LAG-(your phone number)- numbers of broadcast page 1,2,3,4---, the details of all features on the topmost bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc

EOPg (Evidence Of Post, Group)

This is the screenshot showing that an advert has been posted in a group, the following feature should be capture in EOPg, Number of participients in that group, Name or Logo of the group, date when the group was created, the advert shown in the media bar. the details of all features on the topmost bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc

EOPs (Evidence Of Placement, Status)

This is the screenshot showing that an advert has been placed on the broadcaster's status, the following feature should be capture in EOPs, the adverts shown in the status window, the details of all features on the topmost bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc, a maximum of 5 slides is allowed.

EOS (Evidence Of Seen)

This is the screenshot showing and confirming the number of broadcaster's audience who have received and seen the advert, the following feature should be capture in EOS, "Delivered to", "Number of Remaining", actual Date and Time captured in the last received audience, eg Read: August 22, 21:57, the dividing line between Delivered to and Remaining, then the details of all features on the topmost bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc. EOS is to be sent 2 days after the broadcast, this will allow maximum numbers of broadcasters audience to have received and viewed the advert.

EOVgroup (Evidence Of View, Group)

This is the screenshot showing and confirming the number of group participant who have viewed the posted advert. the following feature should be capture in EOV group, "Delivered to", "Number of Remaining", Date and Time captured in the last received participant, eg Read; September 11, 09:57 the dividing line between Delivered to and Remaining, then the details of all features on the topmost bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc

EOVstatus (Evidence Of View, Status)

This is the screenshot showing the numbers of friends who visited the broadcaster's status and viewed the advert. the following feature should be capture in EOVstatus, the adverts shown in the status window, showing respective numbers of viewers per advert, the date shown as yesterday. the details of all features on the topmost bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc, EOVstatus should e sent before the 24 hours status duration lapse.
