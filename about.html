Why MMA

MAA stands for Mobile Advertising Agents, an advertising firm currently building an advertising community to be made up of broadcasters, clients, audience and agent, with the aim and objective of promoting and advertising members businesses by broadcasting / posting / sharing using the most widely use and effective social media platform: Whatsapp.

The community already have over 200,000 members throughout the country and this is expanding geometrically on daily bases. The driving force for this expansion is the scheme introduced by MAA, where broadcasters and agents are paid for sharing information on WHATSAPP. Presently MAA is the only firm that pay you for such service, we believed data can be effectively use to promote ones business or others business (advertisement) and get paid than using the data for non profitable activities..

One of the benefit Of MAA is that every member of MAA community is entitle to a discounted advert rate on his or her business, besides that, a BROADCASTER -get paid for using its data to promote and advertise goods, products or services of other members of the community,

Why MAA Pay You As a smart phone user with active whatsapp account, no doubt you have friends (contacts) who are equally having active whatsapp account too, if MAA uploaded an advert and you download and share the ad to other whatsapp friends on your phone, you are surely advertising MAA clients business, in doing this you're using your expensive data to promote others businesses, MAA pays you for the data used, the payment is a function of numbers of your friends who see the ad and the ad rate. the clients already make payment to MAA for such advertising service.


How Does MAA Gets Its Money


For every advert services run by MAA for a client, we have a fix percentage as commission billed into the service charge, to be borne by the client. We pay tax, bank commission and charges and we maintain staff and our systems. the service charge paid by client is a function of ad rates, bouquet, ad type, ad platform etc. eg if a client want a 5000 audience (ie bouquet) for an advert rate of 1.5, (banner), the client will pay (5000 x 1.5) + 25% of (5000 x 1.5). the 25% is for MAA while the (5000 x 1.5) is for broadcasters who distributed the adverts to their friends, each broadcasters get 1.5 x No of friends who received and see the advert.

Terms used in maa

  • General Terms

    SHARE: The act of distributing information on social media. in whatsapp, it can be splitted into Broadcasting, Posting and placement on Status

    BROADCASTING: The act of distributing information to many audiences at once.

    POSTING: The act of distributing information to members of a whatsapp group.

    PLACEMENT: The act of distributing information to friends through Whatsapp Status.

    A BROADCASTER: A certify member of the MAA community who share paid and/or non-paid ad to contacts (audience) on his/her phone and get paid for such services

    AUDIENCE (Recipients): Active whatsapp account owners in a broadcaster’s phone contact.

    EVIDENCE OF BROADCAST (EOB); A screen shot proving the broadcaster broadcasted the downloaded Advert.

    EVIDENCE OF POST group (EOPg); A screen shot proving the broadcaster posted the downloaded Advert to a group.

    EVIDENCE OF PLACEMENT status (EOPs); A screen shot proving the broadcaster placed the downloaded Advert on her Status.

    EVIDENCE OF SEEN (EOS): A screen shot showing numbers of broadcaster’s audiences (recipients) who actually recieved and see the broadcasted ad.

  • Other Terms in MAA
    EVIDENCE OF VIEW group (EOVg): A screen shot revealing the numbers of participants who viewed the advert posted in a group

    EVIDENCE OF VIEW status(EOVs): A screen shot revealing the numbers of viewers who viewed the advert on the broadcaster's status

    EVIDENCE OF CONTACTS (EOC): a screen shot showing the actual numbers of contact on a broadcasters phone with active whatsapp account.

    CLAIM: Payment entitlement after every paid ad is shared. This is a function of numbers of the broadcaster’s audience who actually see the ad after two days of broadcast, posting or within 24 hours as the case may be.

    ADVERT PLATFORM (AP) MAA have three platforms to share an ad, by broadcasting, by posting in a group or by placing on broadcaster’s status. Broadcasters have the option of participating in any of the platform.

    CLAIM VALUE (CV): Amount calculated by MAA account department or the broadcaster for the service of sharing.

    TIME, DATE OF BROADCAST (TDOB); Typing the time and date in which the broadcast was made, this is type below the EOB screenshot before sending, time is 24 hours e.g. 18 Aug 2019, 16:27. Every member is expected to change their phone time setting to 24 hour.

    TIME, DATE OF POST group (TDOPg); Typing the time and date in which the posting was made, this is type below the EOPg screenshot before sending, time is 24 hours e.g. 18 Aug 2019, 16:27. Every member is expected to change their phone time setting to 24 hour.

    TIME, DATE OF PLACEMENT status (TDOPs); Typing the time and date in which the placement was made, this is type below the EOPs screenshot before sending, time is 24 hours e.g. 18 Aug 2019, 16:27. Every member is expected to change their phone time setting to 24 hour.
