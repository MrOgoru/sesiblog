Posting on a Group

POSTING AD IN A GROUP

This is the process require to share an advert to participants in a whatsapp group in a broadcaster's phone. below are the process and steps accordingly, with illustrative screenshot


Posting an Advert to a group

(1) Download a running advert from the running ad page
(2) Open the group chat page.
(3) Post the advert to the partricpants in the group.
(4) Send your Evidence Of Post group (EOPg) and Time Date Of Post group (TDOPg) immediately, see image.
(5) After two days of Posting, send your Evidence Of View group (EOVg) and Claim Value (CV), see image.

EOPg (Evidence Of Post, Group)

(1) Open the group chat page
(2) Select the posted Ad
(3) Click on the info of the ad
(4) Screenshot and send.
(5) Ensure you type TDOPg under the EOPg before you send.

This is the screenshot showing that an advert has been posted in a group, the following feature should be capture in EOPg, Number of participients in that group, Name or Logo of the group, date when the group was created, the advert shown in the media bar. the details of all features on the topmost and bottom most bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc

EOVg (Evidence Of View, Group)

(1) Open the Group Chat page
(2) Select the posted Ad
(3) Click on the info of the ad
(4) Scroll to the middle of the page, ie the dividing line between
to and Remaining,
(5) Tap on the last participant to reveal the actual date and time
(6) Screenshot and send.
(7) Ensure you type CV under the EOVg before you send.

This is the screenshot showing and confirming the number of group participant who have viewed the posted advert. the following feature should be capture in EOVg, "Delivered to", "Number of Remaining", Date and Time captured in the last received participant, eg Read; September 11, 09:57 the dividing line between Delivered to and Remaining, then the details of all features on the topmost and bottom most bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc
