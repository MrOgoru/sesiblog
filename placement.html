PLACING AD IN STATUS

This is the process require to share an advert by placement on broadcaster's whatsapp status. below are the process and steps accordingly, with illustrative screenshot


Placing an Advert in a status page

(1) Download a running advert from the running ad page
(2) Open your whatsapp status page.
(3) Place the advert on your status.
(4) Send your Evidence Of Place status (EOPs) and Time Date Of Place status (TDOPs) immediately, see image.
(5) Second day, within 24 hours after Placement, send your Evidence Of View status (EOVs) and Claim Value (CV), see image.

EOPs (Evidence Of Place, status)

(1) Open the status page
(2) Select a maximum of 4 Paid Ads and 1 Non Paid Ad, place them on the status page
(3) Click Menu (...) to reveal details of the placed advert
(4) Screenshot and send.
(5) Ensure the total numbers of slides in your status page is not more than 5 before you send.

This is the screenshot showing that an advert has been placed on the broadcaster's status, the following feature should be capture in EOPs, the adverts shown in the status page, the details of all features on the topmost and bottom most bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc, a maximum of 5 slides is allowed.

EOVg (Evidence Of View, Group)

(1) Open the status whatsapp page
(2) Click Menu (...) to reveal details of the placed advert
(3) Screenshot and send.
(4) Ensure you type CV under the EOVs before you send.

This is the screenshot showing the numbers of friends who visited the broadcaster's status and viewed the adverts. the following feature should be capture in EOVstatus, the adverts shown in the status window, showing respective numbers of viewers per advert, the date shown as yesterday. The details of all features on the topmost and bottom most bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc, EOVstatus should e sent before the 24 hours status duration lapse.
