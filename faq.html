Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is MAA

    MAA stands for Mobile Advertising Agents, an advertising firm currently building an advertising community to be made up of broadcasters, clients, audience and agents, with the aim and objective of sharing (advertising) members businesses by using the most widely use and effective social media platform: Whatsapp.

  • Benefit Of Joining MAA

    • One of the benefits of joining MAA is that every member of MAA community belongs to a business community of over 200,000 members scattered nationwide. besides that, every member viz:

      • A BROADCASTER -(1) get paid for using its data to promote and advertise goods, products or services of other members of the community, (2) Is entitled to a discounted advert rate on his or her business, (3) Access to an average of 6 paid ad per week.


      • An AGENT -have a certain commission for facilitating client for MAA
      • An AUDIENCE -get update of goods, services and products within its locality at the most affordable price
      • A CLIENT -have its products, goods and services shared and delivered to the public smart phones nationwide, making his/her business -GO VIRAL.

  • Basic Requirement for Joining MAA

    • A smart phone with a whatsapp account and screenshot function
    • A minimum of 50 contacts also with active whatsapp account
    • A business, service, products or skill you want people to know. (optional)
    • A bank account
  • How does client know total numbers of audience who sees her advert

    MAA compile all the EOS, EOVstatus, EOVgroup screenshots and send this to the client email address, the screen shots contain numbers of audiences, viewers who viewed, recieved and see the advert.

  • How do i create a broadcast group / page

    In your whatsapp home screen,
    (1) select New Broadcast (NOT NEW GROUP). see sample in screenshot page
    (2) Select contacts of your choice to the page; they are your audience who will receive the ad. Maximum of 256 recipients per page, you can create more than one page.
    (3) Click ok, to see the numbers of recipients selected and other details.
    (4) Click to edit the name of the page to “MAA-State-your phone number-page number”
    (5) Screen shot and save in your phone gallery, this is your MAA broadcast page with your phone number as reference.
    (6) The MAA broadcast page (see sample in screenshot page) should reveal the following,
    (i) your phone number,
    (ii) numbers of audience (recipients)
    (iii) your state.
    (iv) page number
    (7) We advise you assign names or nick names to your contact,

    MAA is not interested in the GSM numbers of your friends and colleagues

  • How do i make money by broadcasting an advert

    (1) Download any running advert on your phone
    (2) Place the advert in the broadcast page you created and broadcast
    (3) All your audience (recipients) will receive the advert.
    (4) Send your EOB screenshot (see sample on screenshot page) and type below TDOB, (eg TDOB 18 Aug 2020, 16:27).
    (5) 2 days after broadcast, send in your EOS screenshot and your Claim Value (CV).
    (6) Payment will be made into your bank account within 24 hours.

    PLEASE ENSURE
    Your recipients also save your contact in their respective phone, else any recipient who did not save your number in their phone will NOT receive the advert.

  • How do make money by posting an advert in a group

    (1) Download any running advert on your phone
    (2) Post the advert to any group of choice in your phone
    (3) All participant (members) of the group will see the advert.
    (4) Send your EOPgroup screenshot (see sample on screenshot page) and type below TDOPg, (eg TDOPg 18 Aug 2020, 16:27)
    (5) 2 days after posting, send in your EOVgroup screenshot and your Claim Value (CV).
    (6) Payment will be made into your bank account within 24 hours.

  • How do i make money by placing an advert on my status

    (1) Download any running adverts on your phone
    (2) Place the adverts on your status
    (3) Your friends will have to visit your status to view the adverts
    (4) Send your EOPstatus screenshot (see sample on screenshot page) and type below TDOPs, (eg TDOPs 18 Aug 2020, 16:27)
    (5) Before 24 hours lapse on status after placement, send in your EOVstatus screenshot and your Claim Value (CV).
    (6) Payment will be made into your bank account within 24 hours.

    PLEASE ENSURE
    Total number of slides on your status should be maximum of 5, this includes 1 Non Paid Ad from MAA.

  • What is TDOB, TDOPgroup, TDOPstatus

    Time Date Of Broadcast eg TDOB 18 Aug 2020, 16:27.
    Time Date Of Post group eg TDOPg 18 Aug 2020, 16:27.
    Time Date Of Place status eg TDOPs 18 Aug 2020, 16:27.

    Details showing date and time when broadcast, posting or placement are done, braodcaster should type in these detail to accompany the respective EOB, EOPgroup, EOPstatus respectively. Time is 24 hours as shown above, Every member is expected to change their phone time setting to 24 hour.

    what is Claim Value (cv)

    The is the amount entitle to a broadcaster after sharing adverts,

    For broadcast, CV is (Audiences - Remaining) x ad rate
    For Posting in group, CV is (Participants - Remaining ) x ad rate
    For Placement on status, CV is (viewers within 24 hours) x ad rate.

    MAA advise every broadcaster calculate its claim and send it forward for prompt claim verification, reconciliation and payment.
    MAA is not responsible for delay resulting in third bank transaction.
    OR
    DELAY in payment of CLAIM resulting in broadcasters failure to submit the following respectively in proper order as at when due
    (i) EOB / EOPgroup / EOPstatus and TDOB / TDOPgroup / TDOPstatus immediately after the sharing
    (ii) EOS (2 days after broadcast)/ EOVgroup (2 days after Posting) / EOVstatus (within 24 hours ) and Claim Value accordingly(CV)

    How do i create EOS Screenshot

    (1) Open the broadcast page and select the broadcasted advert
    (2) Click on info symbol of the advert
    (3) Scroll down the info page to the middle where you see a divide with the inscription, "delivered to", and "remaining".
    (4) Click on the last audience to reveal the date and time.
    (5) Screenshot the page and save in your phone gallery as EOS of broadcast

    Please Note

    EOS should be sent after two days of broadcast, this will enable larger numbers of audience to see the broadcasted advert before you send the screenshot. Ensure all the features are captured, see sample in screenshot page.
    How do i create a EOVgroup Screenshot

    (1) Open the group page and select the posted advert
    (2) Click on info symbol of the advert
    (3) Scroll down the info page to the middle where you see a divide with the inscription, "delivered to", and "remaining".
    (4) Click on the last participant to reveal date and time.
    (5) Screenshot the page and save in your phone gallery as EOVgroup

    Please Note

    EOV should be sent after two days of posting, this will enable larger numbers of participant see the posted advert before you send the screenshot. Ensure all the features are captured, see sample in screenshot page.
    How do i create a EOVstatus Screenshot

    (1) Open your status home page
    (2) Click on the three dot (...) ie the Menu
    (3) This will reveal the numbers of viewers, who viewed the placed adverts.
    (4) Screenshot the page and save in your phone gallery as EOVstatus

    Please Note

    EOV should be sent within 24 hours after placement, this will enable larger numbers of friends see the placed advert before you send the screenshot. Ensure all the features are captured, see sample in screenshot page.

  • How Does MAA Gets Its Money

    For every advert services run by MAA for a client, we have a fix percentage as commission billed into, as service charge, to be borne by the client. We pay tax, bank commission, charges and we maintain staff and our systems. The service charge paid by client is a function of ad rates, bouquet, ad type, ad platform etc.
    eg if a client want a 5000 audience (ie bouquet) for an advert rate of 1.5, (banner), the client will pay (5000 x 1.5) + 25% of (5000 x 1.5). the 25% is for MAA while the (5000 x 1.5) is for broadcasters who shared the adverts to their whatsapp friends, each broadcasters get 1.5 x No of friends who received and see the advert.
