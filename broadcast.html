Broadcast Procedure

This is the process require to share an advert to a selected list of friends in a broadcaster's phone. below are the process and steps accordingly, with illustrative screenshot

create a broadcast page

Select New Broadcast from your Whatsapp home page menu

Select recipients (audience) you want to send advert to, the maximum a page can accomodate is 256, you may have to creat another broadcast page if you have more than 256 friends to send advert to.

Click ok, to see this page for details of the broadcast page, numbers of recipients (audience) selected etc.

Click the pencil symbol to edit the page name to reflect your whatsapp numbers, your state and the number of the page, eg MAA-LAG-09037716114-1. in the example we have 173 recipients.

YOU HAVE SUCCESFULLY CREATED A BROADCAST PAGE. this is require to broadcast adverts time to time, the page is flexible as audience can be added to an existing group and more broadcast pages can be created.

EOB (Evidence Of Broadcast)

(1) Open the broadcast page
(2) Select the broadcasted Ad
(3) Click on the info of the ad
(4) Screenshot and send.
(5) Ensure you type TDOB under the EOB before you send.

This is the screenshot showing that a broadcast has been made, the following feature should be capture in EOB, Number of recipients (Audience), the advert shown in the media bar, your MAA reference number ie MAA-LAG-(your phone number)- numbers of broadcast page 1,2,3,4---, the details of all features on the topmost and the bottom most bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc

EOS (Evidence Of Seen)

(1) Open the broadcast page
(2) Select the broadcasted Ad
(3) Click on the info of the ad
(4) Scroll to the middle of the page, ie the dividing line between Delivered to and Remaining,
(5) Tap on the last recipient to reveal the actual date and time
(6) Screenshot and send.
(7) Ensure you type CV under the EOS before you send.

This is the screenshot showing and confirming the number of broadcaster's audience who have received and seen the advert.
The following feature should be capture in EOS, "Delivered to", "Number of Remaining", actual Date and Time captured in the last received audience, eg Read: August 22, 21:57, the dividing line between Delivered to and Remaining, then the details of all features on the topmost and bottom most bar on the screen, ie time, network signal showing data active etc. EOS is to be sent 2 days after the broadcast, this will allow maximum numbers of broadcasters audience to have received and viewed the advert.

Broadcast an advert

(1) Download a running advert from the running ad page
(2) Open the broadcast page your created above.
(3) Send the advert to the recipients in the broadcast page.
(4) Send your Evidence Of Broadcast (EOB) and Time Date Of Broadcast (TDOB) immediately, see image.
(5) After two days of broadcast, send your Evidence Of Screen (EOS) and Claim Value (CV), see image.
